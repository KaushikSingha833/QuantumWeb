About Us

The Quantum Club at C. V. Raman Global University is a student-led initiative dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of Quantum Computing, Quantum Mechanics, and related emerging technologies. Our mission is to create awareness, encourage research, and foster collaboration among students and faculty.
